About Dingzhou Hongyue Hardware Products Co.,Ltd.

ABOUT COMPANY

    Dingzhou Hongyue Hardware Products Co.,Ltd. is located in South Shahe Bridge---Wire Netting Market of Dingzhou, which is one of the largest wire mesh shopping malls and the largest base for the production of Hexagonal Wire Netting and Square Mesh with convenient traffic in China. There is only 300 kilometers away from the port of Tianjin, while is 40 kilometers away from Shijiazhuang Airport.Our company is one of the leading manufacturers with varied wire and wire mesh in China, which was established in 1989.
    Our company possesses advanced equipment, as well as strong technology strength, and stable product quality, which occupies more than 10, 000 square meters. Our company is mainly engage in the manufacture and sales of Hexagonal Wire Mesh, as well as Square Hole Wire Mesh, Welded Mesh, Chain Link Fence, Stainless Steel Wire Mesh and all kinds of PVC Coated Mesh.

ABOUT PRODUCT

    We also supply Galvanized Wire, Black Annealed Wire, Tomato Support, Barbed Wire, PVC Wire, etc, which with dozens of kinds and thousands of specifications.The products are mainly used for animal bred in captivity, construction, transportation, tele-communication, food processing, etc.
    Our products are mainly exported to US, UK, Australia, Europe, India as well as other countries and areas. The products are welcomed by the customers at home and abroad. The yearly output is 18, 000, 000 square meters, while the production value is more than 80, 000, 000 RMB.
    We have won the prize of Contracts Honor and Ex-quality Products. We always stick to the principles of standard quality, prompt delivery and customer satisfaction. We sincerely welcome friends at home and abroad with our cooperation and common development of Hardware market.
