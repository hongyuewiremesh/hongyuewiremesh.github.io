General storage cage - standard storage cage

Storage cage is a kind of international logistics container, which has a similar effect with the tray. Its function has gone deep into the field of production, circulation and consumption, experienced a temporary storage, packaging, handling, storage, transportation and other sectors, runs through the whole process of logistics. Facilitate the warehouse inventory can be stacked on top of each other to four layer height, realize the three-dimensional storage, the use of strong steel bars welded into, make the structure more strong, simple operation, wide application, long service life.
